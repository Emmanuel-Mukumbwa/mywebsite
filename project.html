Hostel Management System

This Hostel Management System is designed to digitize and streamline the management of hostel operations. It includes modules for booking, payments, maintenance, and fault reporting, making it easier for hostel owners to manage their properties efficiently.

Feature 1: Online Booking

Allow students to book rooms online with real-time availability status and instant confirmation.

Feature 2: Digital Payments

Facilitate secure, hassle-free payments through various online payment methods integrated into the system.

Feature 3: Maintenance Tracking

Track maintenance requests and tasks, allowing hostel owners to address issues quickly and effectively.

Feature 4: Fault Reporting

Provide an easy-to-use fault reporting system where students can report issues directly to management.

Feature 5: User Management

Manage student and staff profiles, track accommodations, and monitor usage statistics in one platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a room?

You can book a room by logging into the system and checking room availability under the 'Bookings' section.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and mobile money services like M-Pesa.

How can I report a fault?

To report any faults, simply go to the 'Maintenance' section in your dashboard and submit a fault report.

Can I extend my stay in the hostel?

Yes, you can request an extension via the 'Booking' section, subject to availability of rooms.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

Go to the 'My Bookings' section, find your active booking, and click 'Cancel'. Cancellation policies may apply.
